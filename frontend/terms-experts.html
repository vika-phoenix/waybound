Tour Listings

Travel Experts may create Listings. You must indicate the following information about the Tour in the Listing:

  1. Tour dates, itinerary, its day-by-day schedule, maximum number of participants for each Tour;
  2. Description of additional services (if any);
  3. List of equipment and clothing necessary for the Tour or provided during the Tour;
  4. Tour-specific rules of conduct;
  5. List of destination-specific risks or dangers, including but not limited to language barriers, weather conditions, sanitary, and epidemiological conditions;
  6. List of destination-specific rules and regulations that may directly affect Travelers, including but not limited to visa requirements, medical documentation (including proof of vaccination), prohibited items (including medication);
  7. Traveler's ability to obtain medical insurance and terms and conditions of such insurance;
  8. Refund and cancellation terms in case of Tour cancellation by Traveler after the cooling-off window has closed (30 minutes from booking if departure is more than 7 days away; 15 minutes if departure is 7 days or less away).

Booking and Financial Terms

The Travel Experts, not Waybound, are solely responsible for honouring any confirmed bookings and making available any Tours reserved through the Platform. The Listing for a Tour will specify the Tour Cost.

Once a Tour has been purchased, a Traveler's booking cannot be changed by you or cancelled with a refund, unless otherwise stated in these Terms. The platform grants every Traveler a penalty-free cooling-off window at the moment of booking (30 minutes if departure is more than 7 days away; 15 minutes if 7 days or less away) during which they may cancel for a full refund — this rule cannot be overridden by Travel Experts.

Operator cancellations (platform rule — non-negotiable): If you cancel a confirmed booking for any reason within your control, the Traveler is entitled to a full refund of all amounts paid. You may not impose any cancellation fee or withhold any portion of the Tour Cost when you initiate the cancellation. Repeated cancellations by a Travel Expert may result in suspension or removal from the Platform.

If a Traveler has cancelled the requested booking, or if the Travel Expert declines the Traveler's booking request due to non-availability, Waybound will automatically cancel any pre-authorisation to the Traveler's credit card and/or Travel Expert will refund any amounts charged to the Traveler's credit card in connection with the requested booking within a commercially reasonable time. The refund is done using the same payment method. In the event of credit cards which are charged monthly, the amount will be credited at the end of the current invoicing period.

Payment structure: Waybound uses a two-part payment model. Travelers pay a deposit at the time of booking (the percentage is set by the Travel Expert on their listing). The remaining balance is due by a balance due date calculated from the departure date, also set by the Travel Expert. If the balance is not received by the due date, the Travel Expert may cancel the booking in accordance with the applicable cancellation policy. There are no monthly instalment plans on this platform.
