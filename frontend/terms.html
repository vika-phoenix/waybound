Cancellations and Refunds

Cooling-off window (platform rule — cannot be changed by operator): Every booking includes a short penalty-free cancellation window from the moment of booking. If your departure is more than 7 days away, you may cancel within 30 minutes of booking for a full refund. If your departure is 7 days away or less, the window is 15 minutes. After this window closes, the tour's cancellation policy applies.

Waybound default cancellation policy: Unless the operator has set a custom policy on their listing, the following tiers apply based on how many days remain before departure at the time of cancellation:

  • 30 or more days before departure: Full refund.
  • 14–29 days before departure: 50% refund of total tour cost.
  • 0–13 days before departure: No refund.

Operators may set a custom cancellation policy on their listing, which will be shown to you before you pay. Custom policies may be stricter or more generous than the default. The policy snapshotted at the time of your booking is the one that applies to you, regardless of any later changes by the operator.

Children's pricing: By default, children aged 2–17 are charged at 85% of the adult price. Operators may set a specific child price on their listing, which will override this default. This is shown on the tour detail page before booking.

If the operator cancels (platform rule — cannot be overridden): If a Travel Expert cancels your confirmed booking for any reason within their control, you will receive a full refund of all amounts paid, regardless of the cancellation policy or how close to departure the cancellation occurs.

If the operator changes material tour details: If an operator makes significant changes to a confirmed booking (such as price, meeting point, accommodation, or cancellation policy), you will be notified by email and given a penalty-free cancellation window to cancel for a full refund. The window length depends on how close to departure the change is made.

Force majeure: If a tour is cancelled due to natural disasters, government travel restrictions, or verifiable extreme weather, Waybound will work with the Travel Expert to offer an alternative date or a refund, subject to the specific circumstances.

Refund timeline: Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method. Payment card refunds typically take 5–10 business days to appear, depending on your bank.
