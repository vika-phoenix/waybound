How to use Waybound

At Waybound.com you'll find tours across Russia, Georgia, Turkey & Kazakhstan, bookable online. Work directly with travel experts, skip the agencies, and discover these destinations the way locals do.

What we offer

How it works

Our travel experts offer you personalised trips that feel nothing like a packaged tour. Every route is plotted based on their experience and knowledge of hidden locations — so you discover the world the way locals do.

Our travel experts offer you:
  • Unconventional routes plotted from personal experience and knowledge of the most hidden locations, so you get to know the world in a new way and become familiar with local culture
  • Dream trips planned down to the minute detail with all activities custom-fitted to your needs and interests
  • The chance to reset in the company of your soulmates — people you can identify with
Why Waybound over everything else?
  • We vet each travel expert prior to posting their tours on the website — we assure the safety of your travel and hand-pick the highest quality small group tours
  • You can contact a travel expert directly and ask all pertinent questions about travel details
  • Our search algorithm selects the most suitable travel options according to style, comfort, and activity
  • Our goal is to ensure you get the most affordable price with no extra fees or charges, staying in direct contact with the tour organiser
  • You can read verified reviews from real travelers who have been on the exact tour you're interested in
Step by step

How do you book a tour?

1
Step 1
Select your destination and dates
Use our search to find trips by destination, travel dates, or activity type. Use the filters to narrow down by duration, group size, difficulty, and price. Select one or several tours that appeal to you.
🔍 Search📅 Choose dates🎛️ Use filters
2
Step 2
Study the tour profiles
Read feedback details and learn about the travel expert. Find out exactly what is and isn't included in the cost of the tour. Review the day-by-day programme and select the one that suits you most.
📋 Read itinerary⭐ Check reviews✅ What's included
3
Step 3
Contact your travel expert
Press "Write to the Travel Expert" on the tour page. Ask all pertinent questions before committing. If you'd like to talk, you can request the expert to call you back using our voice communication option.
💬 Message guide📞 Request a call
4
Step 4
Activate messaging for prompt communication
Activate any suitable messenger service in the upper corner. Once your travel expert confirms your booking and has covered all travel details, you can proceed directly to payment from the chatbox — or press "Get to Payment" on the tour page.
💬 In-app chat✅ Confirm details
5
Step 5
Provide traveler details and pay
Fill in the details of all travelers and select your payment method. Payments are processed securely via a bank server using the CVV2 code. You will be charged no extra cost — only the price quoted by the travel expert.
💳 Secure payment🔒 CVV2 protected
✓
Step 6
Receive confirmation and prepare
Booking details are emailed to you and saved to your profile. Before you fly, print: your flight fare (obtained by you), the booking confirmation letter from the website, and a medical insurance policy. Make sure you've agreed on a meeting point with your travel expert before departure.
📧 Confirmation email📄 Print documents🛫 Ready to go!
