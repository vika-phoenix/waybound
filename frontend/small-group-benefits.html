The difference

Small group vs. large group tours

Every tour on Waybound is unique to the travel expert — it adapts not only to the most appropriate season in the country, but to the desires of a specific traveler group. Our experts cover the route themselves more than once and know every detail before offering it to you.

Key benefits

What you actually get

📋
A packaged programme, cleared in advance
You get a detailed program for each day and can clear up all details with your travel expert before departure — no surprises on the ground.
🗺️
Real depth of a country
You'll get to know the length and width of the country and feel like a resident — not a tourist ticking boxes from a bus window.
🤝
Your kind of people
You'll set out on an exciting trip with a small group of new friends — and find soul mates and people you can actually identify with.
🔄
Structured spontaneity
Flexible itineraries leave plenty of room for unique experiences. Your guide adapts in real-time — you're never locked into a rigid schedule.
✅
Guaranteed departures
Every confirmed departure runs — period. No minimum group size surprises, no last-minute cancellations from our side.
🔒
No surprise costs
The price quoted by the travel expert is the price you pay. We don't add our own extra fees or hidden charges on top of the tour cost.
How to choose your small group tour
  • Select the tour fitting your development aspirations and interests — yoga, study, surfing, wildlife, and many more
  • Depending on your needs, choose the tour based on the activity scale — tours range from relaxing to extreme
  • Write directly to several travel experts and ask all pertinent questions before committing
  • Read all feedback from previous participants and review photo reports — real verified reviews from travelers
  • Small group tours available at Waybound.com across Russia, Georgia, Turkey & Kazakhstan — everyone will find a tour to their liking
