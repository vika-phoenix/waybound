Bookings & Reservations
How does booking a tour on Waybound work?
+
Booking is a three-step process:
  • Find your tour — browse tours in Russia, Georgia, Turkey, or Kazakhstan and pick a departure date.
  • Pay the deposit — secure your spot by paying the deposit (percentage set by the operator). Your spot is not confirmed until the deposit is received.
  • Operator confirms — the Travel Expert confirms your booking within 48 hours. You'll get an email with all details.
The remaining balance is due before departure (exact due date shown on your booking). See How it works for the full walkthrough.
Which countries does Waybound cover?
+
We currently operate in four countries: Russia, Georgia, Turkey, and Kazakhstan. Every tour is run by a local Travel Expert who lives and guides full-time in that destination. We plan to expand over time.
How long does the operator have to confirm my booking?
+
After you pay your deposit, the Travel Expert has 48 hours to confirm your spot. If they don't confirm in time, your booking is automatically cancelled and your deposit is fully refunded. You'll be notified by email.
Can I book for a group or join solo?
+
Both are welcome. When booking you specify the number of adults and children — the price is calculated automatically. Solo travellers are very common and guides are experienced at making everyone feel part of the group. All tours have a maximum of 15 participants. See Small group benefits.
What is the price for children?
+
By default, children aged 2–17 are charged at 85% of the adult price. Individual operators may set a stricter rate where child costs are comparable to adults. The exact child price is always shown before you pay. For children under 2, contact the operator directly.
What does "guaranteed departure" mean?
+
A guaranteed departure means the tour will run on that date regardless of how many people have booked — even if you are the only person. Look for the "Guaranteed" badge on tour listings. This gives you certainty when booking flights and accommodation.
How do I contact my guide before the trip?
+
Once your booking is confirmed, use My Messages to contact the operator directly. You can also send an enquiry before booking from any tour listing page. Most operators respond within 24–48 hours.

Payments & Pricing
Is my payment secure?
+
Yes. All card and SBP payments are processed through YooKassa, a fully licensed Russian payment provider. We never store your card details. See Trust & Safety for full details.
What payment methods are accepted?
+
  • Bank card (Visa, Mastercard, Mir) — processed securely via YooKassa
  • SBP (Система быстрых платежей) — instant transfer via QR code
All charges are in Roubles. If a tour is priced in another currency, the Rouble equivalent is shown before you confirm.
How does the deposit and balance system work?
+
Waybound uses a two-part payment model:
  • Deposit — paid at booking to secure your spot. Percentage set by the Travel Expert, shown clearly before you pay.
  • Balance — remaining amount, due by a balance due date set by the operator. Reminder emails are sent as the date approaches.
For last-minute bookings, the operator may require the full amount upfront.
When is my card charged?
+
Your card is charged immediately when you submit the deposit — this is a real charge, not a pre-authorisation. If the operator doesn't confirm within 48 hours, you will be refunded in full automatically.

Cancellations & Refunds
Can I cancel right after booking and get a full refund?
+
Yes — every booking includes a cooling-off window immediately after you pay. This is a platform rule operators cannot override:
  • Departure more than 7 days away: 30 minutes to cancel for a full refund.
  • Departure 7 days or less away: 15 minutes.
After the window closes, the tour's cancellation policy applies.
To cancel, go to My Bookings and select "Cancel booking".
What is the standard cancellation policy?
+
Waybound's default (applies if the operator hasn't set a custom one):
  • 30+ days before departure: Full refund.
  • 14–29 days before departure: 50% refund.
  • 0–13 days before departure: No refund.
Operators may set stricter or more generous tiers — always check the specific policy on the listing.
Full policy in Terms of Use — Cancellations.
What if my tour gets cancelled by the guide?
+
If a Travel Expert cancels your confirmed booking for any reason within their control, you receive a full refund of all amounts paid. This is a non-negotiable platform rule. Refunds are processed within 5 business days. See Trust & Safety.
What if the operator changes tour details after I've booked?
+
If a Travel Expert makes a material change (itinerary, price, dates, inclusions), you will be notified by email and given a penalty-free window to cancel for a full refund — even if the standard policy would normally apply a penalty.
What counts as force majeure?
+
Natural disasters, government travel bans, or verifiable extreme weather qualify for a full refund regardless of policy. Contact viktoriia.strazhnikova@gmail.com with your booking reference and documentation.
How long do refunds take?
+
Approved refunds are processed within 5 business days and returned to your original payment method. You'll receive a confirmation email once initiated. Actual arrival time depends on your bank.

Tours & Guides
Who are the Travel Experts?
+
All guides on Waybound are local, full-time professionals based in Russia, Georgia, Turkey, or Kazakhstan. They live in the destination and guide as their primary work — not agencies or resellers. Every guide is verified before being listed.
How are guides verified?
+
Every Travel Expert goes through identity verification, experience review, and — for adventure, mountain, and trekking tours — confirmation of a valid first aid certificate. Guides are reviewed annually. See Trust & Safety.
What difficulty levels are available?
+
  • Easy — suitable for most fitness levels.
  • Moderate — some walking; comfortable fitness required.
  • Challenging — good fitness and sometimes prior experience needed.
  • Expert — demanding; specific skills or high fitness required.
Always read the requirements on the tour listing before booking.
Do I need travel insurance?
+
We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance covering trip cancellation, medical emergencies, and evacuation. It is your responsibility to arrange this — Waybound does not provide insurance. For adventure tours, ensure your policy covers the specific activities involved.

Group Size
What is the maximum group size?
+
All tours on Waybound have a maximum of 15 participants. This is a platform-wide rule — operators cannot list tours for larger groups. Small groups mean more personalised attention, better access to remote locations, and a richer overall experience. See Small group benefits.
Why does Waybound cap groups at 15?
+
Large groups change the nature of an experience — harder to manage safely in remote areas, disruptive to local communities, and guides can't give adequate attention to each person. At 15 or fewer, a guide knows everyone by name, can adjust the pace, and can access places off-limits to larger tours. It's a quality decision, not a commercial one.
Can I book a private tour?
+
Some Travel Experts offer private departures (just your group). These are marked on the listing. If there's no private option shown, send the operator a message from the tour page — many guides are happy to discuss custom arrangements.

Safety & Trust
What is the Waybound Guarantee?
+
The Waybound Guarantee covers you in three situations:
  • Your guide cancels for any reason within their control → full refund.
  • The tour is significantly different from what was advertised → full refund or free rebooking.
  • There is a verifiable safety issue on tour → full refund.
All refunds processed within 5 business days. See Trust & Safety.
How do I report a problem?
+
Contact us at viktoriia.strazhnikova@gmail.com or +7 928 078-24-98 with your booking reference and description of the issue. For urgent on-trip problems, call directly. You can also use our contact page.
Is my personal data protected?
+
Yes. We only share information necessary to complete your booking with the Travel Expert. We never sell your data to third parties. Read our full Privacy Policy.
Fraud warning — what should I watch out for?
+
Waybound will never contact you asking for payment via WhatsApp, Telegram, or email wire transfer. If someone claims to represent Waybound and asks for off-platform payment, do not pay and report it immediately via our contact page.

For Travel Experts
How do I list a tour on Waybound?
+
Create a Travel Expert account at Sign up as a guide. Once verified, create your first tour from the operator dashboard. New tours go through a review before going live — typically within 48 hours. Visit the operator page for full details.
What commission does Waybound charge?
+
Waybound charges a 15% commission on the Tour Cost. There are no listing fees or upfront costs — you only pay when you earn. Commission is deducted before the balance is remitted to you.
Can I set my own cancellation policy?
+
Yes, within limits. You can set tiers stricter or more generous than the Waybound default. Two rules cannot be overridden:
  • Every tourist gets a cooling-off window at booking (30 min if departure >7 days; 15 min if ≤7 days).
  • If you cancel a confirmed booking, the tourist receives a full refund — no exceptions.
What if I need to cancel a confirmed booking?
+
If you cancel a confirmed booking for any reason within your control, the tourist is entitled to a full refund of all amounts paid. You may not withhold any portion of the Tour Cost. Repeated cancellations may result in suspension from the platform. See Terms for Travel Experts.
Can I change tour details after tourists have booked?
+
Non-material updates (photos, description wording) can be made at any time. If you change material details — itinerary, price, dates, inclusions — tourists with confirmed bookings are automatically notified and given a penalty-free cancel window. Admin is also notified.

Account & Profile
How do I create an account?
+
Go to Sign up and register with your email, or use Google, Yandex, or VK sign-in. You'll receive a confirmation email — click the link to verify before you can book.
I forgot my password — how do I reset it?
+
Click "Forgot password" on the Sign in page. Enter your email and we'll send a reset link. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact us.
How do I view or manage my bookings?
+
All your bookings are in My Bookings. From there you can view details, pay your balance, message your guide, cancel (subject to policy), and leave a review after your trip.
How do I leave a review?
+
After your tour ends you'll receive a review request by email. You can also leave a review from My Bookings on any completed booking. Only verified bookers can leave reviews.
How do I delete my account?
+
Request deletion from Account Settings. If you have active bookings, those must be resolved first. For data deletion requests email viktoriia.strazhnikova@gmail.com with the subject "Account deletion request".
